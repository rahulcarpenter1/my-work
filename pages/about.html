About DRV Events

13+ Years of Excellence in Event Management

Our Story

DRV Events is the fastest-growing event company and wedding planner in Madhya Pradesh, India. Founded in 2013 by Mr. Dhruv Prakash Solanki, we have transformed over 500 celebrations into unforgettable memories.

What started as a passion for creating beautiful celebrations has grown into a full-service event management company. Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and personalized service has made us the preferred choice for weddings and events throughout Dewas and surrounding regions.

We specialize in comprehensive event management, offering services from Photography and Videography to Decoration, Catering, Entertainment, and Complete Event Coordination. Every event we manage is treated as a unique opportunity to create magic and lasting memories.
