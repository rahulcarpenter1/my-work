📝 How to Add Your Instagram Posts

To display your latest Instagram posts from @drvevents on this gallery:

  1. Go to https://www.instagram.com/drvevents
  2. Find the post/video you want to embed
  3. Click on the post and copy the URL (e.g., https://www.instagram.com/p/ABC123DEF/)
  4. In the happy-customers.html file, replace the data-instgrm-permalink values with your post URLs
  5. Save and refresh the page - Instagram embeds will automatically load!

Tip: Mix videos and photos from @drvevents for a dynamic gallery. The Instagram embed script automatically detects and displays the content type.
