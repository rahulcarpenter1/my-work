About DRV Events

13+ Years of Excellence

DRV Events is the fastest-growing event company and wedding planner in Madhya Pradesh, India. With our expertise spanning over 13 years, we've transformed over 500 celebrations into unforgettable memories.

Founded by Mr. Dhruv Prakash Solanki, our company started providing exceptional services since 2013. We offer comprehensive one-stop wedding services including Photography, Videography, Decoration, Catering, Light, Sound, Tent, Documentation, Choreography, Band Baja, and Orchestra.

Beyond weddings, we manage Birthday Parties, Anniversary Celebrations, School & College Functions, and are rapidly expanding into the Corporate sector with services like Product Launches, Team Building Activities, Customer Meets, Conferences, and Product Branding.
